      How is this calculated?

      1. Time Calculation:
      This tool multiplies your playcount by the track's duration.
      Example: Listening to a 10-minute song once counts as more time than listening to a 3-minute song three times (9 mins). That's why a track with fewer plays might rank higher!

      2. API Limitations:
      Due to Last.fm limits, we fetch your top 1,000 tracks to calculate these stats. If you have a huge library, tracks outside this top 1,000 aren't counted in the total time, but the estimate is usually very accurate for your top charts.
